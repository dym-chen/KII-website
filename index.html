OUR STORY

Kii is the first brand to introduce a modern twist on the tea culture which originated from Asian. To perfectly match the selected quality tea's from across Asia, we have decided to add a subtle, but enjoyable alcoholic taste. Our fruity series is another inspiration of tea, we select the quality and in-season organic fruits globally which is infused within our freshly brewed tea.

"Go above and beyond" has always been our spirit and we are dedicated to provide our custormers the new concept of the healthiest and freshest tea!
